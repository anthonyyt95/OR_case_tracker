📷 What happens to the diagram?

    
When you save to Google Sheets

    

      The diagram is captured as a PNG image and handled differently depending on which Apps Script you're using:
    

    

      
✓ apps_script_v2.gs (recommended)

      
The diagram is automatically uploaded to a folder called "OR Case Log Images" in your Google Drive. A clickable link appears in the sheet — tap it to open the image.

    

    

      
⚠ apps_script_v1.gs (original)

      
The raw image data is stored as a very long text string in the cell — not human-readable. Upgrade to v2 for proper image handling.

    

    
You can also always use 🖨 PDF to save a full printable snapshot of the case including the diagram.
